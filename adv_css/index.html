Nintendo

Nintendo is a Japanese video game company founded in 1889 that's best known for creating some of the most iconic franchises in gaming history. It produces popular consoles like the Nintendo Switch and most popular characters such as Mario, Zelda and Pokemon. Nintendo is known for designing both video game hardware and software, including iconic consoles such as NES, Super Nintendo, Wii and the most recent console the Nintendo Switch.

Nintendo Switch

In 2017, Nintendo has released a hybrid gaming console. It can be used as a home console when docked to the TV or as a portable handheld with a built-in screen. The Switch features its detachable joy-con controllers, multiple play modes such as TV, tabletop or handheld. The Switch's system software supports online gaming through internet as well as local wireless with other consoles.

Nintendo Switch 2

In 2025, Nintendo has realeased the next generation hybrid gaming console of the original of the Nintendo Switch. The difference between Switch 2 then the first one is that it has a much stronger performance, a bigger screen and new online/social features. The Switch 2 is the newest gaming console that Nintendo has released since 2017, it has different features compared to the original switch like much higher graphic capability, stronger battery,bigger screen,faster displays, online social features like voice and screen chat. Since its launch, the Switch 2 has become one of Nintendo's fastest-selling console, selling millions around the world.
